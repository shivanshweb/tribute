About Elon Musk

  • 1971 - Elon Reeve Musk was born on June 28, 1971 (age 50) Pretoria, South Africa
  • 1997- Graduated with bachelor of Science degree in economics and a Bachelor of Arts degree in physics.
  • 1995 - In 1995, Musk, Kimbal, and Greg Kouri founded web software company Zip2 with funds from angel investors.
  • 1999 - Musk co-founded X.com, an online financial services and e-mail payment company.The startup was one of the first online banks to be federally insured, and, within its initial months, over 200,000 customers joined the service.
  • 2002 - In 2001, Musk became involved with the nonprofit Mars Society. With $100 million of his early fortune, Musk founded Space Exploration Technologies Corp., traded as SpaceX.
  • 2008 - Tesla, Inc.—originally Tesla Motors—was incorporated in July 2003 by Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning,Musk assumed leadership of the company as CEO and product architect in 2008. A 2009 lawsuit settlement with Eberhard designated Musk as a Tesla co-founder, along with Tarpenning and two others.
  • 2008 - Tesla first built an electric sports car, the Roadster, in 2008. With sales of about 2,500 vehicles, it was the first serial production all-electric car to use lithium-ion battery cells.
  • 2016 - In 2016, Musk co-founded Neuralink, a neurotechnology startup company to integrate the human brain with AI.
  • 2013 - In 2013, Musk announced plans for a version of a vactrain, assigning a dozen engineers from Tesla and SpaceX to establish the conceptual foundations and create initial designs.On August 12, 2013, Musk unveiled the concept, which he dubbed the Hyperloop.
